About Second Hand Guitar Pedals

Second Hand pedals are available from guitarguitar. Every store accepts trade ins so there will always be an interesting selection available.

We get a wide range of pedals traded in, from affordable pedalboard stalwarts to some interesting and rare items. We've had wild, weird and wonderful effects pedals come in from all corners of the globe: effects aficionados will do well to keep an eye on the site!

Pre-Owned stock moves quickly so if you see a pedal that takes your fancy, don't delay! Check out our entire range of second hand guitar pedals on our site or chat to the staff in any guitarguitar store.

Frequently Asked Questions about Second Hand Guitar Pedals

Question: Is every pedal tested before being traded in?

Yes. Every pedal (every item in fact, not just pedals) is carefully appraised and tested before being accepted and put up for sale. Details about condition, packaging, manuals etc will be included in the item's unique description.
